# Fidelity Levels

| Level | Conceptual Goal | Aerodynamics | Weights | Propulsion | Geometry | Tail Sizing | Sizing Loop | Mission Analysis |
|-------|----------------|--------------|---------|------------|----------|-------------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Level I** | Cannot distinguish between F-18 and F-35 | Type-based Cf and K_LD lookup; K2 from LD_max; K1 = 0 | Historical: We/Wto = a·Wto^c (Raymer Table 6.1) | Type-based TSFC; density-ratio thrust lapse | Roskam S_wet regression; Roskam L_fus regression | Not called | `SizingLoopL1`: iterates W_TO; S_ref and T_SL are outputs | Roskam fuel fractions for fixed segments; single-point Breguet (tabulated LD, TSFC) for cruise/loiter |
| **Level II** | Can distinguish aircraft within a category (fighter vs. transport, etc.) | CD0 = Cf·Swet/Sref; K2 = 1/(π·e·AR) with Oswald e; K1 = 0 | Gross empty weight fraction: We/Wto = f(Raymer Eq 6.1) | Mattingly installed TSFC; density-ratio thrust lapse | Roskam S_wet + Raymer exposed-wing S_wet; computed cbar and L_fus | Volume coefficient method (Raymer Eq 6.28–6.29); single-point HT and VT areas | `SizingLoopL2`: iterates W_TO and T_SL; S_ref is fixed input; tail sized each iter | Single-point Breguet per segment using aero.drag_polar and prop.TSFC |
| **Level III** | Can meaningfully compare 4 concepts and arrive at a PSC | Component drag build-up (Cf_turb from Reynolds number); CLmax from lift-curve slope; K1 non-zero for cambered wings | Component build-up: wing, fuselage, tail, LG, engine, subsystems | Mattingly dry/wet lapse; installed TSFC from Mattingly correlations | Explicit component planform dimensions; S_wet_fus, S_wet_wing, S_wet_HT, S_wet_VT from geometry tables | Same volume coefficient method using detailed geometry inputs | `SizingLoopL2` (same code); higher-fidelity discipline inputs improve accuracy | Sub-segmented cruise/climb (20 sub-segments default); L/D recomputed per sub-segment weight |
| **Level IV** | Research-grade; not required for AOE 4065 | Level III + wave drag correction for M > 1 (Raymer Ch 12) | Raymer Ch 15 component weights: Eq 15.1 wing, 15.2–15.3 tail, 15.5–15.24 subsystems | Same as Level III; adds Raymer Ch 10 engine sizing (scale_engine) | Same as Level III | Same volume coefficient method | `SizingLoopL2` with L4 discipline objects | Same as Level III; higher-fidelity inputs from L4 aero/prop |
